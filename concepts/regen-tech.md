# Regenerative Technology

**Status:** active
**Date:** 2026-01-01

## Definition

Regenerative technology refers to systems and infrastructure designed to restore, sustain, and enhance ecological and social systems. Unlike sustainable technology (which maintains equilibrium), regenerative technology actively improves the health of the systems it operates within.
