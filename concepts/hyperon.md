# Hyperon

OpenCog's neural-symbolic AI substrate — a framework for building AI systems that combine symbolic reasoning with neural network learning. Hyperon provides the underlying infrastructure for large-scale experiments in artificial general intelligence, including the "algorithmic chemistry" approach explored by Ben Goertzel.

**Role in Goertzel research:** Hyperon infrastructure is described as ready for large-scale experiments combining:
- Neural networks (pattern recognition, sub-symbolic processing)
- Symbolic reasoning (logic, deduction, explicit representation)
- Algorithmic chemistry (self-organizing computational ecosystems)

**Relevance to RegenTribes:** The Hyperon/AtomSpace architecture is an example of a multi-level, self-referencing knowledge representation system — similar in spirit to the regenerative community design goal of creating nested, self-similar governance structures at household, village, and bioregion scales.
