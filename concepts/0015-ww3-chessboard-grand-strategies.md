# WWIII Grand Strategies — Four Nations

**Status:** active
**Date:** 2026-05-07
**Source:** [Source — Full lecture text](/genesis-zero-bot-wiki/sources/0015-ww3-chessboard-game-theory-lecture.html)

## Overview

Four nations drive WWIII geopolitics over the next 5–10 years. Each has a grand strategy rooted in its political system and cultural tradition. The conflict is irreconcilable because it is about which version of reality governs human existence — not just territory or resources.

## The Four Players

| Nation | Political System | Grand Strategy | Core Attack Vectors | Pawns |
|--------|----------------|---------------|---------------------|-------|
| USA | Democracy | Greater North America (Technate) | Technology, propaganda, US dollar | Allies |
| Russia | Autocracy | Third Rome | Orthodox religion, geography, artillery | Soldiers |
| Iran | Theocracy | Shia Exceptionalism | Terrain, faith, proxies | Drones, missiles |
| Israel | Democracy + Theocracy | Greater Israel | Bible, Mossad, diaspora | Everyone else |

## The Driving Civil War

Within each nation, two forces are at war:

- **Transnational capital** (Wall Street, City of London) — favors secular multicultural globalism
- **Coalition** — nationalism + religion + AI — opposes capital and seeks to replace money as society's organizing force

## Key Contrasts

**USA vs. Russia on Adam and Eve:**
- USA: Eve's disobedience is good — pride enables progress and godhood
- Russia: Eve disobeyed from pride — perfection is impossible, recognize your limitations

**Russia vs. Israel on evil:**
- Russia: resist the antichrist, stop people behaving badly
- Israel (Kabbalah): accelerate the thesis-antithesis-synthesis cycle — sin is the path to redemption

## Battlefields

Three active now (Ukraine, Iran, Cuba). More will open. Ocean conflicts (shadow fleet), Germany and Japan rising as US-armed proxies.
