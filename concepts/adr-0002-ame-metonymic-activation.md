# ADR 0002 — AME Metonymic Activation and Virtual Trust Field

Documents structural isomorphism between AME (Affinity Mapping Engine, Mythogen framework) and the linguistic sign model from Radden & Kövecses (2017).

## Core Mapping

| Article (Trapezium Model) | AME Equivalent |
|---------------------------|----------------|
| Linguistic form | Detected seed element |
| Concept | Living Seed (whole person) |
| Experienced projections | V-crystal, maturity, bloom cycles |
| Relationships | Affinity links, FOT indicators |
| Virtuality | FOT = virtual field, inferred from proxies |
| Metonymy | One detected element activates whole seed |

## Article Core Claim

> "it means by virtue of the linguistic form activating (virtually) the entire trapezium-like configuration of forms, concepts, experienced projections, and relationships between all of the above. Activation of the real world remains dubious or indirect."

## AME Anti-Capture Alignment

AME deliberately does NOT directly measure the real world. It measures **expression patterns** (count, resonance, emotional density) — all **virtual activations**, not direct measurements. Same principle as the article.

## Key Insight

The FOT (Field of Trust) is a **field** in the physics sense — not a score, not a number. It is inferred from 5 behavioral indicators whose minimum defines the field strength. The "hologram principle": one indicator off, the whole field degrades.
