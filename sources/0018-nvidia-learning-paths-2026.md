# NVIDIA Training and Certification Learning Paths 2026

Published May 2026 by NVIDIA Corporation. Author: Kehong Lu.

## What This Document Is

A comprehensive guide to NVIDIA's training and certification ecosystem. Organised by audience (Developers vs. Infrastructure Professionals) and by topic. Each learning path lists individual courses with duration and cost.

## Certification Tiers

NVIDIA offers two certification tiers:

- **Associate** — entry-level. One-hour exam. Digital badge. Cost: USD 125.
- **Professional** — advanced. Two-hour exam. Digital badge. Cost: USD 200–400.

All certifications are based on NVIDIA hardware and software solutions.

### Domain-Specific Training Tracks

- **OpenUSD** — 3D scene description. Foundation for industrial digital twins. All courses free. Professional cert available (USD 200).
- **Robotics** — Isaac ROS / Isaac Lab / Omniverse. Heavy simulation-to-reality focus. 28-hour Robotics Foundations learning path, all free.
- **Physical AI** — NVIDIA Cosmos. Training physical-world AI agents in simulation.
- **Industrial Digital Twins** — Omniverse-based. USD 30–90 courses. Combines OpenUSD + Physics + AI.
